A Guide to Demon Slaying

「 KNYDEX 」


「 The King of Demons 」

The Demon Slayer Corps have been fighting demons for almost 1,000 years in hopes of finally defeating the demon king, Muzan Kibutsuji. To end this relentless war, we need your help. KNYDEX is a guide to teach and inform you about the slayers, demons, and breathing techniques before you start your training. Using KNYDEX, we will also decide the breathing technique that is best suited for you. Remember that this journey will be a rough one, but you are far more capable than you think.
